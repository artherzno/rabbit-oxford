SIPHO MALABA

Managing Director, Africa & Head of Financial Transformation Practice

Sipho Malaba is the Managing Director of Oxford Acuity in Africa and leads the Financial Transformation practice group. Sipho has over 20 years’ experience in providing financial services to a wide array of clients. His key areas of expertise are assurance and consulting in banking, developmental funding institutions, stockbrokers, retail banking, investment banking and securities firms. Sipho has been active in South Africa, Botswana, Zimbabwe, Mozambique, Kenya, Nigeria, Uganda, Malawi and Mauritius.

Prior to joining Oxford Acuity, Sipho was a partner in a Big 4 professional services firm, where he lead the firm’s Financial Services division and sat on the Executive Committee for South Africa.

In the past, Sipho managed the Internal Audit division for Sub Saharan Africa for a global banking group and subsequently a listed Financial Risk Services company in Johannesburg.

Sipho is a Chartered Accountant (South Africa) and Chartered Accountant (Zimbabwe) and an MBA from the University of Oxford. Sipho also holds a Postgraduate diploma in Banking from the Rand Afrikaans University. He has also attended Executive Development programs with Insead University and Harvard University.

Sipho spends his spare time contributing to the causes of underprivileged in Africa and is based in Johannesburg, South Africa.
