BUSINESS CONSULTANT

BANGKOK

RESPONSIBILITIES :

  • Must have functional area manager experience
  • Must have working knowledge of the defense business system (DBS) investment certification requirements and processes
  • Must have working knowledge of the planning, programming, budget, and execution (PPBE) process
  • Must have working knowledge of the programming and budget systems (e.g. Program Budget Information System (PBIS), Program Budget Information System-IT (PBIS-IT), Select & Native Programming Information Technology (SNaP-IT), DoD Resources Data Warehouse (DRDW))
  • Must have working knowledge of the Defense Information Technology Portfolio Repository (DITPR) or DITPR-DON
  • Must have excellent written and oral communication skills, to be able to effectively advise senior government personnel
  • Must have experience in policy development
  • Must be an skilled (power user) in Microsoft Office tools (i.e. Word, Excel, PowerPoint, Access, and Visio) and Adobe Pro
  • Must be able to work independently or with limited supervision
  • Must possess critical thinking, problem solving, and initiative/proactive skills
  • Location is at the Pentagon
  • Want an Active Secret Clearance

BENEFITS :

  • A world-class brand
  • The opportunity to earn an exceptional income
  • Expert training and marketing support
  • Opportunity to work with other experts on projects
  • Low overhead costs/the freedom to shape your own business
